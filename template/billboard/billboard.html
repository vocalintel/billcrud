OBJECTIVE

The major objective of our outdoor advertising company is to select,erect and service billboards at various locations as agreed with the clients. The responsiblity of selecting ensures that the locations are strategic and viable for the audience being targeted. The placement of the billboard is a configuration of its size and the existing environment features. Usually, yhe visual attraction of the billboard in terms of their aesthetic values should greatly enhance the beauty of the urban scene.The erection of such boards normally will not pose immediate of future problems to the draibnage network no encroach on the walkway(s), public safety and transportation

 

MARKETING

The client of an outdoor advertising industry could either be an advertising agency or a manufacturing company or marketing company as the case may be. The client rents a billboard from the outdoor advertising company for a specific period of time , usually one year. The rentals contract is formally renewed at expiration. Rental rates for conventional outdoor advertising billboards such as 40 sheet,10 sheet,bulletin boards, street directional signs and 4 sheet are usually determined and regulated by the Outdoor Advertising Association of Nigeria.(DAAN)
Thermal rates for unique billboards designed and introduced by an individual company within the association are isuallly determined by the company that introduces such unique boards. e.g -Keesha Billboard introduced the 'Spectacles' and the rental rates are determined by Keesha Billboard only.
